HotDogs

This is just a good old fashion hotdog. You can never go wrong with this food especially after a long day in the sun. For when you are hungry but don't feel like cooking.In this receipe we can either cook the hotdogs in hot water or we can grill them over heat

In this receipe we will need:

  • Choice of HotDogs
    • Chicken
    • Turkey
    • Beef
  • Buns
  • Condiment
    • Ketchup
    • Mustard
    • Relish

Now that we have all our ingrediants, Let's cook.

  1. We are going to have to cook the hotdogs. You can either boil in water or over the grill
  2. Get your buns and start toasting them once the hotddgs are almost done
  3. Assemble your hotdogs and add all the condiments you desire.

Enjoy your meal!
